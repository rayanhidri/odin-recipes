RECIPE FOR A VERY DELICIOUS LASAGNA

INGREDIENTS

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato
  • Sugar
  • Spices
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheese
  • Eggs
  • Love

STEPS

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the Lasagna
  5. Cover with foil and bake
  6. Let the lasagna rest before serving it
