RECIPE FOR A VERY DELICIOUS HAMBURGER

INGREDIENTS

  • Ground chuck
  • Crashed crackers
  • Worcestershire sauce
  • Egg
  • Milk
  • Seasonings
  • Cheese

STEPS

  1. Combine ingredients into a bowl.
  2. Make four patties.
  3. Grill the patties until the juices run clear and the meat is no longer pink in the center.
